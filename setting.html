About

Real Estate Price Estimation System is a comprehensive platform designed to streamline property management, lead tracking, and price estimation for real estate professionals.

Our system provides powerful tools to help you manage your real estate business efficiently, from tracking leads and managing tasks to generating accurate price estimations and detailed reports.

Key Features:

  • Advanced price estimation algorithms using linear regression
  • Comprehensive property management
  • Task tracking and organization
  • Detailed reporting and analytics
  • User-friendly dashboard
